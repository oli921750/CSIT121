Reflection of My Tier List of Perfumes

This tier list ranks my favorite perfumes based on how I would list them according to my admiration for them. I love them all, but for the purpose of this lab assignment, I decided to categorize them into different tiers. Doing so highlights my preferences among all perfumes. The tier list is ranked from one to five, with one being my most favorite. The tier list is in an order that reflects which perfumes I would purchase first or receive as gifts. I learned that restraint, reduction, and emphasis are three fundamental principles to keep in mind to avoid losing someone's interest and possibly losing users.
